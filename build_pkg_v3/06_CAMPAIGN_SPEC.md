# Campaign Specification

## Act I — Landfall

### Mission 1 — First Lattice

Faction: Vanguard

Teaches:

- selection;
- movement;
- fog;
- harvesting;
- lattice;
- power;
- turret;
- first raid;
- Shard Bloom.

Objectives:

1. establish command zone;
2. harvest 500 Aether;
3. place six lattice tiles;
4. build Power Node;
5. build turret;
6. survive raid;
7. investigate relay.

Optional:
contain bloom without losing a harvester.

### Mission 2 — Claim the Red Field

Faction: Forge

Teaches:

- red shards;
- overdrive;
- salvage;
- storm timer;
- aggressive expansion.

### Mission 3 — Voices in the Dust

Faction: Phantom

Teaches:

- stealth;
- detection;
- sabotage;
- capture;
- non-destructive objective.

## Act II — Escalation

### Mission 4 — The Worm Road

- convoy;
- thumpers;
- worm avoidance/luring.

### Mission 5 — Furnace Line

- siege;
- power overload;
- environmental pressure.

### Mission 6 — Borrowed Machines

- hijack;
- false contacts;
- mixed objectives.

## Act III — Revelation

### Mission 7 — Stabilization Protocol

- large base;
- moral choice;
- suppress or preserve network.

### Mission 8 — The Sleeping Engine

- buried colony machine;
- Titan Worm escalation;
- multi-stage defense.

### Mission 9 — Dominion Shard

- three-faction confrontation;
- final choice;
- ending resolution.

## Endings

1. Extraction
2. Stabilization
3. Convergence
4. Planet
5. Hidden Accord

## Campaign requirements

- briefing;
- objective panel;
- in-mission comms;
- optional objective;
- win/lose;
- debrief;
- progress save;
- unlocks/choices;
- replay.

## Mission acceptance

Every mission must be completable:

- without dev tools;
- on all supported browsers;
- at all intended difficulty levels;
- with no single mandatory hidden action;
- after save/load where supported.
