# Art and Audio Pipeline

## Visual target

Late-1990s pre-rendered RTS readability with modern polish.

Not a direct desert or Dune visual copy.

Aether Prime uses:

- black glass;
- pale mineral dust;
- rust fractures;
- blue-white crystals;
- violet electrical growth;
- deep green-black crust.

## 3D-to-2D unit pipeline

Blender source required.

Per vehicle:

- source `.blend`;
- `.glb`;
- textures;
- 16 directions;
- idle;
- move;
- attack;
- damage;
- destruction;
- shadow;
- icon;
- portrait;
- atlas metadata.

Per infantry:

- 8 directions minimum;
- idle;
- walk;
- attack;
- hit;
- death.

Per building:

- construction stages;
- idle;
- production/active;
- damage;
- destruction;
- footprint;
- icon;
- portrait.

## Temporary art

Allowed until P2 only if:

- correct final size;
- correct atlas format;
- correct silhouette;
- TEMP label;
- replacement ticket.
