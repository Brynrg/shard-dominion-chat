# 07 — Factions, Units, and Buildings

## Vanguard Enclave

Belief: Aether Prime can be stabilized and colonized.

Strengths: reliable combined arms, radar, air support, defensive precision.

Weakness: predictable, relies on power/radar networks.

Unique mechanics: Survey Doctrine, Stabilizer Drop, relay-linked defenses, air corridors.

Core unique units: Longwatch Tank, Aegis Walker, Sky Lance, Relay Crawler.

## Forge Dominion

Belief: survival belongs to whoever can extract, armor, and endure.

Strengths: heavy armor, brute production, siege, salvage.

Weakness: slow, loud, high agitation, power hungry.

Unique mechanics: Overload Foundries, Scrap Reclamation, Molten Lattice, Crawler Armor.

Core unique units: Anvil Tank, Kiln Siege Rig, Furnace Behemoth, Scrap Drone.

## Phantom Collective

Belief: Aether is not a resource; it is a signal.

Strengths: speed, stealth, sabotage, deception.

Weakness: fragile, high micro, weak in straight fights.

Unique mechanics: Signal Veil, Ghost Orders, Hijack Pulse, Silent Harvesters.

Core unique units: Veil Runner, Needle Bike, Hijacker Cell, Echo Prism.
