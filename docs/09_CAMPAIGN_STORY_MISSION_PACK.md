# 09 — Campaign Story and Mission Pack

## Premise

Aether Prime was once a failed colony. The first settlers discovered that Aether Shards could power cities, heal radiation sickness, and store human memories. Then the colony went silent.

Decades later, three factions return after detecting a repeating signal:

> Do not mine the dead. We are still here.

## Major factions

### Vanguard Enclave
Wants to stabilize and recolonize the planet.

### Forge Dominion
Wants to extract enough Aether to secure humanity's industrial survival.

### Phantom Collective
Believes the Aether signal is conscious and wants to merge with or free it.

## Campaign shape

9 missions:
- 3 Vanguard
- 3 Forge
- 3 Phantom

## Mission list

1. **Vanguard — First Lattice**: tutorial. Establish base, harvest, power relay, survive bloom.
2. **Forge — Claim the Red Field**: aggressive extraction. Teach volatile resources and production overload.
3. **Phantom — Voices in the Dust**: stealth infiltration. Teach fog, sabotage, and signal structures.
4. **Vanguard — The Worm Road**: escort harvesters through deep crust. Teach worm counterplay.
5. **Forge — Furnace Line**: hold a siege base while overharvesting a red field.
6. **Phantom — Borrowed Machines**: use hijack tech to turn enemy armor against its owners.
7. **Vanguard — Stabilization Protocol**: discover that over-stabilization suppresses the colony minds inside Aether.
8. **Forge — The Sleeping Engine**: activate buried mining engine; choose between output and catastrophe.
9. **Phantom — Dominion Shard**: reveal the Aether Shards contain uploaded colony consciousness.

## Endings

Extraction Victory: Forge dominance. Humanity survives; planet is damaged.

Stabilization Victory: Vanguard dominance. Colony restored but Aether contained.

Convergence Victory: Phantom dominance. Human/Aether identities merge.

Planet Victory: too much agitation. Aether Prime wipes out all factions.

Hidden Accord: complete optional objectives across factions. Shared survival path.
