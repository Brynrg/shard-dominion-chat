# Shard Dominion — Art Prompts

Use these as starting prompts for image/model generation. Do not include references to specific existing games.

## Vanguard Surveyor
Low-poly sci-fi scout rover, clean angular white and cobalt armor, small sensor mast, wide tires, readable top-down RTS silhouette, black glass alien terrain base, original design, no logos, no text.

## Forge Anvil Tank
Low-poly heavy sci-fi tank, bulky iron armor, ember orange heat vents, industrial mining-war machine, oversized treads, original silhouette, readable from top-down RTS camera, no logos, no text.

## Phantom Veil Runner
Low-poly stealth hover scout, thin asymmetrical black frame, violet and teal signal glow, antenna fins, sleek but fragile, original top-down RTS silhouette, no logos, no text.

## Processor Building
Sci-fi mineral processor building, glowing blue crystal intake, armored industrial base, modular RTS footprint, readable top-down oblique sprite, original design, no text.

## Power Node
Sci-fi power tower with coil rings and blue-white arcs, compact RTS building, strong silhouette, original design, no text.

## Titan Leviathan
Crystalline seismic alien organism, segmented glass-like armor, bioluminescent blue core veins, emerges from cracked black crust, not a sandworm, original design, no text.
