# Shard Dominion Project Plan

Shard Dominion is a retro-modern sci-fi RTS about three factions fighting over Aether Prime, a living mineral planet that reacts to extraction, construction, power emissions, and warfare.

## First playable milestone

Phase D — First Lattice

A Vanguard tutorial mission where the player deploys a base, builds lattice and power, harvests Aether, scouts a relay, survives a shard bloom, and defeats/evades Forge raiders.

## Signature mechanic

Planet Agitation rises from harvesting, deep crust movement, raw-ground construction, power spikes, and combat; it triggers shard blooms, worm sign, Titan Worms, glass storms, and crust collapse.
